Our Story

Happy Car Decor was founded with a simple vision: to transform ordinary vehicles into extraordinary expressions of personal style. Located in the heart of Pokhara at Naya-bazar, we have been serving car enthusiasts with passion and dedication since our inception.

Our journey began when our founder recognized the growing demand for personalized car decoration services in Nepal. What started as a small workshop has now evolved into a premier destination for car decoration, attracting customers from across the region.

We believe that every car tells a story, and our mission is to help you tell yours in the most beautiful and unique way possible. Whether you're looking for subtle elegance or bold statements, our team of skilled artisans and designers work tirelessly to bring your vision to life.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical car decoration project take?

Project duration varies depending on the service. Simple vinyl wraps take 1-2 days, while complete custom paint jobs may take 5-7 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you provide warranty on your services?

Yes! We provide warranties ranging from 1-10 years depending on the service. Paint protection film comes with a 10-year warranty, while other services have 1-3 year warranties.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Absolutely! Visit our shop to see our portfolio, or check our social media pages for the latest projects. We're proud to show off our craftsmanship.

Do you work on all car models?

Yes, we work on all car makes and models, from compact cars to SUVs and luxury vehicles. Our team has experience with various vehicle types.
